Reasonable questions

The fine print, dryly.

Is this a joke?

The figurine isn't. The price is debatable. It's a genuine, hand-finished collectible — produced once, in a numbered run of 20, then retired forever.

Is it actually functional?

It functions as a magnificent paperweight, conversation-ender, and mantelpiece centrepiece. Body-safe, non-porous seal; flared display base. Beyond that, we decline to elaborate — and so, tastefully, should you.

Is it really gold?

24-karat gold electroplate over a solid resin core, hand-polished to a mirror and sealed. The weight is real. The shine is real. The restraint is not included.

What does "limited to 20" mean?

Exactly 20 pieces will ever exist. Each is numbered on its base and certificate. When the final piece ships, the mold is destroyed on camera. There is no second run.

How does shipping work?

Free, fully insured, worldwide — in discreet, unmarked outer packaging. Reservations ship in the order numbers are assigned. You'll get tracking the moment yours leaves the studio.

Returns?

30 days on unopened editions. Open it and you've accepted your number into your life. We think you'll keep it.
